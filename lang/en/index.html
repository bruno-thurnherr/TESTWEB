Home

In the home screen you will find direct jumps to the main functions of our application

Realtime
measurement

With the real-time measurement function, it is possible to display the reception quality of a WLAN to which you have successfully logged in. With this option you have the possibility to place your wireless router or amplifier optimally in the room.

Overview
measurements

In this menu you have direct access to the most important functions for measurement. (Create / Edit / Delete)

Add measurement

Here you can create a measurement for your object for which you want to measure the WLAN level. An object can be a house, apartment or even a company location.

Edit measurement

In the Edit measurement function, you can either rename the name of your measurement, or change existing measurement points, or update the measurement.

Visualization

In the visualization you can see all measuring points for a measurement and turn to all details.
